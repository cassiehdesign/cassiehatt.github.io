Complete Scholar Series

Chalkboard Publishing introduced the Complete Scholar Series, a workbook series consisting of 6 books ranging from preschool to grade 3 content in English and math, each 208 pages long. This project took place over several months, with a strict deadline as all books needed to be printed and in stores by a certain date.

My role on this project was the lead designer, I was in charge of the cover design, layout design and assembling all 6 books. The large files were efficiently created to be shared with other team members. I handled all preflight operations for several different printers, and designed the graphics for various ads and email templates promoting this series.
