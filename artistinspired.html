Artist Inspired Spreads

Artist Inspired is a hobby magazine focusing on different creative topics. They were looking to have 10 new pages designed for their August issue where each spread brought a unique and fun design, highlighting something different.

For this project I was in charge of completely designing the page layouts, and providing a few of the supporting images where needed. It was important to keep certain elements the same for consistency, such as the footer area, while also giving each spread a different look to match the topic. Typography and hierarchy are my priorities for magazine spreads to capture and enhance what each article is communicating.
