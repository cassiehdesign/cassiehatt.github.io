I'm Cassie
Marketing and Design Coordinator with a strong creative vision and experience in multi-channel campaigns, social media, paid & organic marketing, branding, print, and graphic design. Based in Calgary, Alberta.

A Little About Me

With an advanced diploma and over 5 years experience as a full-time marketing and design coordinator, I'm passionate about combining my creative and technical skills to make my campaigns memorable and stand out. I focus on the problem and pride myself on figuring out how my design can be the solution.

I have worked on many different projects across all platforms, collaborating with multiple departments and teams to output the highest quality work that drives results while meeting the client's needs. I thrive off of learning new things and hold onto any opportunity that helps me further my skills. I am passionate and hard-working, and never stop creating.

In my free time, I enjoy playing boardgames (Azul, Wingspan, and Clank are great!), hiking, paddleboarding, painting, crocheting, and relaxing on the couch with my two kitties!
